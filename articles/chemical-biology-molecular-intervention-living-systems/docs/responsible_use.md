# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- clinical diagnosis
- therapeutic decisions
- biological safety decisions
- human or animal experimentation
- regulatory submissions
- hazardous biological work
- validated drug discovery
- genetic or cellular engineering decisions
- laboratory procedures without professional review

Professional chemical biology requires validated probes, trained personnel, biosafety review, assay controls, target validation, uncertainty analysis, data provenance, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
