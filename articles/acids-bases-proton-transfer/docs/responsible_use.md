# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- chemical safety decisions
- medical or clinical decisions
- pharmaceutical dosing
- drinking-water compliance
- wastewater treatment dosing
- industrial neutralization control
- environmental regulatory reporting
- hazardous chemical handling
- laboratory procedures without professional review

Professional chemical practice requires validated data, trained personnel, documented assumptions, calibrated instruments, safety procedures, uncertainty analysis, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
