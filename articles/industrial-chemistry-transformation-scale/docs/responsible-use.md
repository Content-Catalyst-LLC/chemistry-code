# Responsible Use Notes

Industrial chemistry evidence can influence plant design, process safety, emissions, worker exposure, community risk, product quality, and capital investment. The examples in this folder are not validated for those purposes.

## Do not use this folder for

- plant design;
- process safety decisions;
- pressure relief sizing;
- reaction calorimetry conclusions;
- regulatory reporting;
- GMP product release;
- environmental permitting;
- procurement or investment decisions;
- operating instructions.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- validate reaction chemistry, impurity profiles, kinetics, and thermochemistry;
- perform process hazard analysis and management of change review;
- collect reaction calorimetry, vent sizing, compatibility, corrosion, and runaway data where relevant;
- define equipment, control loops, alarms, interlocks, relief systems, and emergency procedures;
- include solvent recovery, waste treatment, emissions, and wastewater data;
- use validated analytical methods and quality systems;
- assess lifecycle, feedstock, decarbonization, circularity, and community impacts;
- distinguish screening models from approved process designs.
