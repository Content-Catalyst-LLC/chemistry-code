# Methodology Notes

This folder models a reproducible industrial chemistry and scale-up workflow using synthetic educational data.

The workflow demonstrates:

1. route-screening records;
2. yield, E-factor, solvent intensity, energy intensity, and space-time yield;
3. batch variation and quality summaries;
4. unit-operation energy, water, and solvent-loss records;
5. hazard-register risk proxies;
6. decarbonization pathway prioritization;
7. lifecycle and responsible-scale review;
8. full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

The examples are teaching workflows, not validated process-development, safety, quality, or plant-design systems.
