# Industrial Chemistry Reproducibility Checklist

- Record route chemistry, stoichiometry, feedstock identity, impurity profiles, and catalyst history.
- Preserve unit operations, process flows, equipment assumptions, and operating windows.
- Track yield, selectivity, conversion, E-factor, solvent intensity, energy intensity, and space-time yield.
- Include batch records, in-process controls, analytical methods, and specifications.
- Document process safety hazards, safeguards, abnormal scenarios, and management of change.
- Track heat release, pressure behavior, gas evolution, corrosion, solids handling, and incompatibilities.
- Record waste, emissions, water, solvent recovery, and recycle streams.
- Include decarbonization, lifecycle, feedstock, and circularity assumptions.
- Distinguish educational screening from validated industrial operation.
