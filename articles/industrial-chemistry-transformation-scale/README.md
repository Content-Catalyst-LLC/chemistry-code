# Industrial Chemistry and the Transformation of Scale

This article folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article **Industrial Chemistry and the Transformation of Scale**.

The workflows use synthetic educational data to demonstrate route screening, yield and E-factor metrics, solvent intensity, energy intensity, space-time yield, separation difficulty, process safety review flags, batch variation, process capability, decarbonization pathways, and full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

## Repository structure

- `python/` — industrial process screening, sustainability metrics, and provenance manifests
- `r/` — batch replicate summaries, yield variation, impurity variation, and capability-like statistics
- `julia/` — process metrics for conversion, yield, E-factor, residence time, and space-time yield
- `fortran/` — compact industrial process metric kernel
- `rust/` — industrial process dataset manifest validator
- `go/` — CSV audit utility for industrial route records
- `c/` — process yield, E-factor, and residence-time utility
- `cpp/` — process route candidate classifier
- `sql/` — schema and queries for routes, batches, unit operations, utilities, hazards, and lifecycle notes
- `docs/` — methodology, reproducibility, and responsible-use notes
- `data/` — synthetic educational industrial chemistry data
- `notebooks/` — notebook scaffolds and report notes
- `outputs/` — generated tables, reports, figures, and manifests

## Responsible-use note

All data in this folder are synthetic and educational. These workflows are not validated for plant design, process safety, process control, regulatory reporting, procurement, environmental claims, GMP release, or industrial investment decisions.
