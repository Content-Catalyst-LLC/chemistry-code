# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- chemical safety decisions
- environmental compliance reporting
- clinical or pharmaceutical speciation decisions
- industrial reactor control
- water-treatment dosing
- hazardous chemical handling
- regulatory submissions
- process optimization without professional review

Professional chemical practice requires validated data, trained personnel, documented assumptions, evaluated thermodynamic constants, activity corrections where appropriate, uncertainty analysis, safety review, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
