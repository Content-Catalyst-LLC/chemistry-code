# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- hazardous reaction design
- chemical safety decisions
- industrial reactor operation
- pharmaceutical stability claims
- environmental compliance reporting
- combustion or explosion modeling
- process-control decisions
- regulatory submissions
- medical or clinical decisions

Professional chemical practice requires validated methods, trained personnel, instrument calibration, uncertainty analysis, safety review, domain expertise, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
