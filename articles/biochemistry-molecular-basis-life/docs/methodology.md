# Methodology Notes

## Purpose

This repository demonstrates reproducible computational scaffolding for biochemistry and the molecular basis of life.

## Core Relationships

Michaelis-Menten kinetics:

v = Vmax [S] / (Km + [S])

Binding equilibrium:

Kd = [P][L] / [PL]

Fractional occupancy:

theta = [L] / (Kd + [L])

Hill-type binding scaffold:

theta = [L]^n / (Kd^n + [L]^n)

Free energy and equilibrium:

Delta G standard = - R T ln K

First-order decay:

A(t) = A0 exp(-k t)

Flux balance scaffold:

S v = 0

Sequence composition:

f_i = n_i / N

## Scientific Caution

These examples use simplified educational assumptions. Real biochemistry requires validated assays, experimental controls, biological context, appropriate units, uncertainty analysis, statistical design, sequence/structure validation, safety review, and expert interpretation.
