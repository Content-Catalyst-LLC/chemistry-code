# Reproducibility Notes

All datasets are synthetic or simplified educational datasets.

Reproducible stoichiometry workflows should preserve:

- balanced reaction equation
- coefficient conventions
- reactant and product identities
- molar masses and sources
- reagent purity assumptions
- measured masses and volumes
- concentration units
- temperature and pressure conditions
- limiting reagent logic
- yield basis
- uncertainty assumptions
- computational scripts
- output artifacts
- software versions
- provenance records
- limitations and responsible-use notes

Future extensions could include:

- chemical equation balancing through linear algebra
- oxidation-reduction balancing
- reaction-network stoichiometric matrices
- material-balance diagrams
- real thermochemical data integration
- laboratory information management system exports
- uncertainty propagation
- process-scale conversion and selectivity workflows
