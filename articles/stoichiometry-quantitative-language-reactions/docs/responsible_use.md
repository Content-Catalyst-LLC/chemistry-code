# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- chemical safety decisions
- reagent dosing in real laboratories
- pharmaceutical preparation
- clinical chemistry
- environmental compliance reporting
- industrial reactor operation
- explosive, toxic, or hazardous chemical preparation
- regulatory validation
- process-control decisions

Professional chemical practice requires validated methods, trained personnel, documented assumptions, appropriate software, uncertainty analysis, safety procedures, material safety data, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
