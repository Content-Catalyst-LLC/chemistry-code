# Responsible Use Notes

These files are educational examples for chemistry communication, computational learning, and reproducible workflow design.

They should not be used as:

- research-grade carbonate-system calculations
- shellfish-hatchery management guidance
- fisheries advisories
- coral-reef management decisions
- climate attribution studies
- regulatory submissions
- legal evidence
- operational ocean forecasts
- public-health advisories

Ocean carbonate chemistry can affect marine ecosystems, coastal economies, fisheries, aquaculture, conservation decisions, and climate interpretation. Real applications require validated measurements, calibrated sensors, appropriate carbonate-system software, qualified ocean chemists, uncertainty analysis, and transparent communication with affected communities.

The examples intentionally use synthetic data.
