# Methodology Notes

This folder models a reproducible surface chemistry and catalysis workflow using synthetic educational data.

The workflow demonstrates:

1. catalyst candidate records;
2. competitive Langmuir coverage calculations;
3. synthetic surface-rate proxies;
4. adsorption isotherm records;
5. replicate conversion and selectivity summaries;
6. deactivation time-series analysis;
7. surface characterization metadata;
8. lifecycle and sustainability review flags;
9. full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

The examples are teaching workflows, not validated catalyst-design or process-engineering systems.
