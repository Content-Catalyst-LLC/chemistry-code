# Surface Chemistry and Catalysis Reproducibility Checklist

- Record catalyst composition, synthesis route, support, pretreatment, and activation.
- Preserve surface area, site density, particle size, porosity, and dispersion records.
- Record adsorption conditions, temperature, pressure, concentration, and coverage assumptions.
- Preserve raw and processed characterization data.
- Report conversion, selectivity, yield, product balance, and uncertainty.
- Check heat and mass-transfer limitations.
- Include time-on-stream, deactivation, regeneration, and poisoning tests.
- Record critical material, toxicity, recovery, and lifecycle assumptions.
- Distinguish screening results from validated catalytic performance.
