# Surface Chemistry, Interfaces, and Catalysis

This article folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article **Surface Chemistry, Interfaces, and Catalysis**.

The workflows use synthetic educational data to demonstrate adsorption isotherms, competitive surface coverage, catalyst screening, active-site metrics, selectivity, deactivation, surface characterization records, interface-energy notes, sustainability flags, and full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

## Repository structure

- `python/` — adsorption coverage, catalyst screening, rate proxies, and provenance manifests
- `r/` — catalyst replicates, selectivity summaries, and deactivation analysis
- `julia/` — Langmuir coverage and rate-proxy calculations
- `fortran/` — compact surface-reaction metric kernel
- `rust/` — surface catalysis dataset manifest validator
- `go/` — CSV audit utility for catalyst records
- `c/` — Langmuir coverage and Arrhenius utility
- `cpp/` — catalyst candidate classifier
- `sql/` — schema and queries for catalysts, adsorption, rates, characterization, and lifecycle flags
- `docs/` — methodology, reproducibility, and responsible-use notes
- `data/` — synthetic educational surface chemistry and catalysis data
- `notebooks/` — notebook scaffolds and report notes
- `outputs/` — generated tables, reports, figures, and manifests

## Responsible-use note

All data in this folder are synthetic and educational. These workflows are not validated for catalyst design, process engineering, environmental claims, safety-critical materials selection, industrial scale-up, or regulatory use.
