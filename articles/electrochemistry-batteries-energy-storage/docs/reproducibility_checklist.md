# Electrochemistry and Energy Storage Reproducibility Checklist

- Record electrode composition, loading, porosity, current collectors, separator, electrolyte, and cell format.
- Preserve formation protocol, cycling protocol, current rate, voltage window, temperature, pressure, and rest time.
- Report charge and discharge capacity, coulombic efficiency, energy efficiency, and impedance evolution.
- Include replicate cells and uncertainty.
- Track degradation, gas formation, lithium plating, resistance growth, and material loss where relevant.
- Document safety review, abuse-test boundaries, and end-of-life handling assumptions.
- Include materials inventory, critical-material flags, and recycling pathways.
- Distinguish cell-level, module-level, and pack-level performance.
- Distinguish screening data from validated deployment evidence.
