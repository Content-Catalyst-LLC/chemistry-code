# Methodology Notes

This folder models a reproducible electrochemistry, battery, and energy-storage workflow using synthetic educational data.

The workflow demonstrates:

1. electrochemical cell candidate records;
2. capacity and energy calculations;
3. coulombic efficiency and cycle-retention summaries;
4. voltage-profile records;
5. impedance-growth review;
6. materials inventory and critical-material flags;
7. lifecycle and recycling review;
8. full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

The examples are teaching workflows, not validated battery-assessment systems.
