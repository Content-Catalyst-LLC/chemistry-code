# Responsible Use Notes

Battery and energy-storage evidence can influence grid storage, electric vehicles, consumer electronics, fire safety, procurement, recycling, and environmental claims. The examples in this folder are not validated for those purposes.

## Do not use this folder for

- battery design or certification;
- electric-vehicle or grid-storage decisions;
- safety, abuse, or thermal-runaway assessment;
- procurement decisions;
- recycling operations;
- shipping or hazardous-waste classification;
- regulatory reporting;
- product qualification.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- define cell format, electrode loading, electrolyte quantity, separator type, and formation protocol;
- report temperature, pressure, current rate, voltage window, rest time, and test equipment;
- include replicate cells and uncertainty;
- track coulombic efficiency, energy efficiency, impedance, gas generation, and voltage hysteresis;
- perform safety, abuse, overcharge, puncture, crush, and thermal testing where relevant;
- document material provenance, critical minerals, and recycling pathways;
- distinguish laboratory screening from validated pack-level performance;
- align chemistry choice with application, lifetime, safety, and lifecycle requirements.
