# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the Geochemistry and the Chemical History of Earth article repository with full-stack computational examples.

## Added languages

- Julia — isotope dating, weathering intensity, redox proxies, and geochemical pressure modeling
- C — low-level radiometric age, isotope-ratio, and weathering-index kernels
- C++ — geologic unit triage and geochemical province aggregation
- Fortran — numerical radioactive decay and parent-daughter isotope evolution
- Rust — safe systems-style geochemical screening and provenance scoring
- Go — lightweight geochemical province and rock-type summaries
- R — statistical summaries for rock type, tectonic setting, isotopes, and chemical weathering

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide mineral exploration advice, reserve estimates, mining decisions, legal evidence, environmental compliance, radiological safety determinations, geohazard forecasts, archaeological authentication, or research-grade geochronology.
