# Molecular Dynamics and Chemical Simulation

This article repository supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article:

Molecular Dynamics and Chemical Simulation

The repository provides reproducible computational scaffolding for molecular dynamics and chemical simulation, including velocity-Verlet integration, Lennard-Jones potentials, Coulomb-energy examples, trajectory summaries, mean-squared displacement, diffusion estimates, radial distribution histogram scaffolds, ensemble metadata, simulation workflow records, SQL-backed molecular-dynamics metadata, notebook scaffolding, and cross-language examples in Python, R, Julia, Fortran, Rust, Go, C, and C++.

No university affiliation is implied by this repository. The design goal is advanced reproducible computational rigor: clear assumptions, transparent calculations, synthetic educational data, provenance records, responsible-use notes, and reusable scientific workflow structure.

## Scientific Focus

The workflows demonstrate:

1. One-dimensional velocity-Verlet integration.
2. Force, mass, acceleration, position, and velocity updates.
3. Lennard-Jones potential calculations.
4. Coulomb-energy scaffolds.
5. Mean-squared displacement.
6. Diffusion coefficient estimation.
7. Radial distribution histogram scaffolds.
8. Ensemble and simulation protocol metadata.
9. Trajectory summary tables.
10. SQL-backed molecular-dynamics metadata.
11. Cross-language numerical reproducibility.

These examples are educational and methodological scaffolds. They are not validated molecular dynamics engines, production biomolecular simulations, drug-discovery systems, materials-certification tools, safety models, regulatory systems, or substitutes for professional computational chemistry practice.
