# Methodology Notes

## Purpose

This repository demonstrates reproducible computational scaffolding for quantitative concepts associated with the Chemical Revolution.

## Conservation of Mass

In a closed system:

sum(m_reactants) = sum(m_products)

## Oxidation Mass Gain

For simplified oxidation:

m_oxide = m_metal + m_oxygen

## Combustion of Carbon

C + O2 -> CO2

## Water Formation

2H2 + O2 -> 2H2O

## Historical Caution

The scripts are educational. They do not reconstruct actual eighteenth-century experiments. They use simplified modern formulas to illustrate the quantitative principles that became central to modern chemistry.
