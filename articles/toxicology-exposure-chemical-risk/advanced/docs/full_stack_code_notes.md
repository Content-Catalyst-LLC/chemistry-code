# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the advanced Toxicology, Exposure, and Chemical Risk repository beyond Python and SQL.

## Added languages

- Julia — probabilistic exposure and hazard-index modeling
- C — low-level chronic daily intake and hazard quotient calculations
- C++ — mixture hazard-index and target-system aggregation
- Fortran — numerical exposure-dose and margin-of-exposure calculations
- Rust — safe systems-style exposure screening and risk indexing
- Go — lightweight scenario and route-level toxicology summaries
- R — statistical target-system and mixture hazard summaries

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide public-health advice, clinical diagnosis, causation analysis, legal evidence, regulatory compliance, occupational safety determinations, product safety claims, drinking-water determinations, site cleanup decisions, or real-world community risk conclusions.
