# Advanced Computational Layer: Food Chemistry and the Molecular Basis of Nutrition

This folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article:

**Food Chemistry and the Molecular Basis of Nutrition**

The code is synthetic and educational. It demonstrates food chemistry and nutrition-science analytics, including nutrient-density scoring, bioavailability-adjusted nutrients, food-matrix effects, protein-quality screening, glycemic accessibility proxies, lipid oxidation vulnerability, processing retention scenarios, food-safety flags, sustainability context, SQL provenance, and responsible-use documentation.

## Responsible-use boundary

This repository does **not** provide dietary advice, clinical nutrition guidance, diagnosis, food-safety certification, allergen clearance, regulatory compliance, product health claims, or substitute interpretation by registered dietitians, food scientists, physicians, toxicologists, public-health professionals, or regulators.
