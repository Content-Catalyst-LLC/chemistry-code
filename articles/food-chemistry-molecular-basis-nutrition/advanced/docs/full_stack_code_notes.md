# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the advanced Food Chemistry article repository beyond Python and SQL.

## Added languages

- Julia — numerical food-matrix and bioavailability modeling
- C — low-level nutrient-density and oxidation calculations
- C++ — structured food-matrix scenario modeling
- Fortran — numerical nutrient-density and retention calculations
- Rust — safe systems-style food chemistry scoring
- Go — lightweight command-line style food chemistry analytics
- R — statistical food-group summaries

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide dietary advice, clinical nutrition guidance, food-safety certification, regulatory compliance, allergen clearance, product health claims, or professional nutrition assessment.
