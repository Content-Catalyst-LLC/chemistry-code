# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- industrial reactor design
- catalyst safety decisions
- hazardous chemical operation
- clinical enzyme assay interpretation
- regulatory submissions
- environmental compliance reporting
- industrial scale-up decisions
- pressure or temperature control decisions
- laboratory procedures without professional review

Professional chemical practice requires validated methods, trained personnel, catalyst characterization, kinetic validation, safety review, uncertainty analysis, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
