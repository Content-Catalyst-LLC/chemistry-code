# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the Atmospheric Chemistry and Climate Processes article repository with full-stack computational examples.

## Added languages

- Julia — greenhouse forcing, ozone formation, aerosol optical sensitivity, and atmospheric pressure modeling
- C — low-level radiative-forcing, ozone, aerosol, and lifetime kernels
- C++ — atmospheric class triage and climate-process aggregation
- Fortran — methane lifetime decay and first-order atmospheric persistence modeling
- Rust — safe systems-style atmospheric chemistry screening
- Go — lightweight atmospheric monitoring and chemical-class summaries
- R — statistical summaries for greenhouse gases, aerosols, ozone chemistry, and reactive species

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide climate attribution, air-quality compliance, public-health advice, emissions inventory validation, chemical transport modeling, regulatory reporting, legal evidence, wildfire smoke guidance, or operational atmospheric forecasting.
