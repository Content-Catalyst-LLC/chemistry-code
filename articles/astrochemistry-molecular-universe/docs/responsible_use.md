# Responsible Use Notes

These files are educational examples for chemistry communication, computational learning, and reproducible workflow design.

They should not be used as:

- professional molecular line identifications
- exoplanet atmospheric retrievals
- mission-analysis products
- biosignature assessments
- astrobiology life-detection claims
- laboratory spectroscopy catalogs
- telescope proposal evidence
- legal or policy evidence

Astrochemical interpretation can influence public understanding of life in the universe, mission priorities, planetary habitability claims, and media narratives. Real applications require qualified astronomers, chemists, spectroscopists, laboratory data, calibrated observations, uncertainty analysis, and careful avoidance of biological overclaiming.

The examples intentionally use synthetic data.
