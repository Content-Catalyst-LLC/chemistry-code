# Methodology Notes

This folder models a reproducible colloids, soft matter, and complex fluids workflow using synthetic educational data.

The workflow demonstrates:

1. colloidal system records;
2. dispersed-phase and continuous-phase metadata;
3. Brownian diffusion estimates;
4. volume-fraction and Einstein-viscosity calculations;
5. rheology replicate summaries;
6. shear-thinning, yield-stress, and aggregation flags;
7. emulsion and gel network records;
8. lifecycle and responsible-formulation review flags;
9. full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

The examples are teaching workflows, not validated formulation-assessment systems.
