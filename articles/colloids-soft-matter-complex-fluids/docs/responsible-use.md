# Responsible Use Notes

Colloids and complex fluids can influence food, medicine, personal care, water systems, paints, coatings, inks, batteries, construction materials, environmental exposure, and industrial processing. The examples in this folder are not validated for those purposes.

## Do not use this folder for

- product qualification;
- food safety claims;
- medical or pharmaceutical formulation decisions;
- cosmetics claims;
- industrial process control;
- environmental safety claims;
- wastewater or exposure assessment;
- regulatory reporting.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- measure particle or droplet size using validated methods;
- include replicate rheology with controlled temperature, protocol, rest time, and pre-shear;
- test storage, freeze-thaw, dilution, salt, pH, temperature, and shear stability;
- preserve formulation composition, mixing history, and processing conditions;
- document surfactants, polymers, additives, preservatives, solvents, and particles;
- evaluate worker exposure during powders, aerosols, sprays, and mixing;
- assess wastewater fate, biodegradation, persistence, and recovery where relevant;
- distinguish screening results from validated product readiness.
