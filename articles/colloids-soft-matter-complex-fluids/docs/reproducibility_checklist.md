# Colloid and Soft Matter Reproducibility Checklist

- Record dispersed phase, continuous phase, concentration, volume fraction, and additives.
- Preserve mixing order, shear history, temperature, pH, ionic strength, and storage conditions.
- Report particle or droplet size distribution, not only a mean.
- Report zeta potential and stability conditions where relevant.
- Document rheometer geometry, temperature, pre-shear, rest time, gap, shear-rate range, and wall-slip controls.
- Include replicate measurements and uncertainty.
- Test stability under realistic storage and use conditions.
- Record lifecycle, exposure, wastewater fate, and additive concerns.
- Distinguish formulation screening from validated performance claims.
