# Colloids, Soft Matter, and Complex Fluids

This article folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article **Colloids, Soft Matter, and Complex Fluids**.

The workflows use synthetic educational data to demonstrate colloidal stability, particle and droplet size metrics, volume fraction, diffusion estimates, rheology screening, shear-thinning behavior, yield-stress flags, salt aggregation, emulsion and gel stability records, and full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

## Repository structure

- `python/` — colloid screening, diffusion estimates, rheology flags, and provenance manifests
- `r/` — replicate rheology summaries and stability flags
- `julia/` — Brownian diffusion, volume fraction, and viscosity metrics
- `fortran/` — compact colloid metric kernel
- `rust/` — colloid dataset manifest validator
- `go/` — CSV audit utility for colloid records
- `c/` — diffusion and Einstein-viscosity utility
- `cpp/` — complex-fluid candidate classifier
- `sql/` — schema and queries for colloids, rheology, stability, emulsions, gels, and lifecycle notes
- `docs/` — methodology, reproducibility, and responsible-use notes
- `data/` — synthetic educational colloid and complex-fluid data
- `notebooks/` — notebook scaffolds and report notes
- `outputs/` — generated tables, reports, figures, and manifests

## Responsible-use note

All data in this folder are synthetic and educational. These workflows are not validated for product qualification, food safety, medical use, environmental claims, industrial process control, cosmetics claims, or regulatory decisions.
