# Responsible Use Notes

Chromatography is widely used in pharmaceutical, environmental, food-safety, forensic, clinical, industrial, and regulatory contexts. The examples in this folder are not validated for those purposes.

## Do not use this folder for

- clinical diagnosis or treatment;
- forensic identification;
- regulatory compliance;
- environmental enforcement;
- pharmaceutical release;
- industrial quality certification;
- hazardous-material identification;
- safety-critical chemical decisions.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- preserve raw instrument files;
- document sample preparation and chain of custody where relevant;
- validate peak integration methods;
- use blanks, standards, internal standards, and quality controls;
- report calibration range, detection limits, quantitation limits, and uncertainty;
- confirm important identifications using spectral evidence and authentic standards;
- track retention-time shifts and system suitability.
