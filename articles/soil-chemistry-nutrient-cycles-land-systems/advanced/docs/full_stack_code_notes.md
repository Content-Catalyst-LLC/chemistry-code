# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the advanced Soil Chemistry, Nutrient Cycles, and Land Systems repository beyond Python and SQL.

## Added languages

- Julia — soil fertility, nutrient balance, and land-system pressure modeling
- C — low-level pH, CEC, nutrient, and leaching kernels
- C++ — soil-zone triage and land-system aggregation
- Fortran — numerical mineralization and nutrient-pool dynamics
- Rust — safe systems-style soil chemistry screening
- Go — lightweight soil-region and land-use summaries
- R — statistical summaries for soil class, nutrient pressure, and land-system indicators

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide agronomic prescriptions, fertilizer recommendations, regulatory determinations, land-management decisions, contamination findings, soil-health certification, ecological risk assessment, legal evidence, or operational monitoring guidance.
