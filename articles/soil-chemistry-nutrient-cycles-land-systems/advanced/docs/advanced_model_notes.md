# Advanced Model Notes: Soil Chemistry, Nutrient Cycles, and Land Systems

This advanced layer is designed to make the GitHub repository more credible as a computational science companion to the article.

## What this layer adds

- soil organic carbon stock estimation
- equivalent soil mass proxy
- cation exchange and base saturation
- exchange acidity proxy
- nitrate leaching vulnerability
- phosphorus export proxy
- annual nitrogen balance
- annual phosphorus balance
- salinity and pH pressure indicators
- contaminant pressure screening
- soil land-system pressure index
- soil carbon scenario modeling
- nitrogen balance scenario modeling
- phosphorus export scenario modeling
- SQL-ready provenance structure
- lightweight tests

## Important simplifications

The workflow is intentionally educational. It does not include:

- equivalent soil mass correction suitable for carbon accounting
- crop-specific nutrient recommendation systems
- soil-test calibration curves
- hydrologic leaching models
- phosphorus transport models
- erosion process models
- contaminant bioavailability models
- uncertainty propagation
- spatial interpolation
- regulatory thresholds by jurisdiction
- field-validated soil health scoring

## Interpretation

The output indices are screening and teaching indicators. They help explain how soil chemistry connects measurements to land-system risks and functions, but they are not scientific findings or management recommendations.

## Responsible-use boundary

Do not use these outputs for fertilizer recommendations, liming recommendations, public-health decisions, gardening safety decisions, land purchase decisions, contamination determinations, carbon-credit claims, legal evidence, or regulatory reporting.
