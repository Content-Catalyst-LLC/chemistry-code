# Molecular Geometry, Symmetry, and Structure

This article repository supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article:

Molecular Geometry, Symmetry, and Structure

The repository provides reproducible computational scaffolding for molecular geometry, symmetry, and structure, including coordinate tables, bond distances, bond angles, torsion angles, distance matrices, centers of mass, simple rotation operations, RMSD calculations, VSEPR metadata, SQL-backed molecular-structure records, notebook scaffolding, and cross-language examples in Python, R, Julia, Fortran, Rust, Go, C, and C++.

No university affiliation is implied by this repository. The design goal is advanced reproducible computational rigor: clear assumptions, transparent calculations, synthetic educational data, provenance records, responsible-use notes, and reusable scientific workflow structure.

## Scientific Focus

The workflows demonstrate:

1. Molecular coordinates and structure tables.
2. Bond distances from three-dimensional coordinates.
3. Bond angles from vector dot products.
4. Torsion-angle and conformer comparison scaffolding.
5. Distance matrices and adjacency matrices.
6. Centers of geometry and centers of mass.
7. Rotation matrices as simple symmetry operations.
8. RMSD between conformers.
9. VSEPR-style geometry metadata.
10. SQL-backed molecular geometry and symmetry records.
11. Cross-language numerical reproducibility.

These examples are educational and methodological scaffolds. They are not laboratory protocols, validated quantum-chemistry packages, chemical safety systems, regulatory tools, molecular design systems, or substitutes for professional chemical practice.
