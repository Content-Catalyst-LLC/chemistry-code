# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the Circular Chemistry, Waste, and Material Futures article repository with full-stack computational examples.

## Added languages

- Python — full circular material screening workflow and reports
- SQL — provenance schema and circular material query examples
- Julia — circular retention and material-flow scoring
- C — recovery yield, circular retention, material loss, and hazard-flow kernels
- C++ — material-stream triage and material-class aggregation
- Fortran — repeated-cycle material loss scenario modeling
- Rust — safe systems-style circular material screening
- Go — lightweight material-class summaries
- R — statistical summaries for material streams and circularity indicators

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not certify recyclability, validate chemical recycling, approve waste handling, determine regulatory compliance, replace life-cycle assessment, evaluate real toxicity, determine food-contact suitability, authorize product claims, or substitute for professional engineering, waste-management, legal, regulatory, toxicological, or environmental review.
