# Mass Spectrometry Reproducibility Checklist

- Preserve raw MS files and converted open-format files where possible.
- Record ionization mode, analyzer, source conditions, scan type, mass range, and calibration.
- Record chromatography conditions when MS is coupled to separation.
- Document peak picking, centroiding, smoothing, alignment, deconvolution, and integration settings.
- Preserve blanks, standards, quality controls, and internal-standard records.
- Report mass error, isotope evidence, retention evidence, and MS/MS fragment evidence.
- Distinguish tentative exact-mass matches from confirmed identifications.
- Store processed data and figures with source provenance.
- Report uncertainty, limitations, and alternative interpretations.
