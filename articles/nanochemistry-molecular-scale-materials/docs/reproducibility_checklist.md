# Nanochemistry Reproducibility Checklist

- Record precursor identity, synthesis route, temperature, solvent, pH, concentration, reaction time, and purification.
- Report core size, hydrodynamic size, size distribution, aggregation state, and measurement method.
- Record ligand shell, surface charge, coating, functional groups, and stability conditions.
- Preserve raw and processed characterization data.
- Characterize materials in relevant media and after storage or exposure.
- Distinguish primary particles from aggregates.
- Include replicate measurements and uncertainty.
- Document critical-material, toxicity, persistence, transformation, and end-of-life assumptions.
- Distinguish screening results from validated application readiness.
