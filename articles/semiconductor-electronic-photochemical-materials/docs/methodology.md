# Methodology Notes

This folder models a reproducible semiconductor, electronic, and photochemical materials workflow using synthetic educational data.

The workflow demonstrates:

1. material candidate records;
2. band-gap and absorption-edge relationships;
3. charge-transport proxies;
4. mobility balance metrics;
5. device-like replicate summaries;
6. photostability time-series analysis;
7. spectroscopy and excited-state metadata;
8. interface and lifecycle review flags;
9. full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

The examples are teaching workflows, not validated device-assessment systems.
