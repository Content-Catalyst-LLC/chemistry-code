# Semiconductor, Electronic, and Photochemical Materials

This article folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article **Semiconductor, Electronic, and Photochemical Materials**.

The workflows use synthetic educational data to demonstrate band-gap screening, photon-energy relationships, charge-transport proxies, photostability analysis, device-like replicate summaries, degradation flags, critical-material review, and full-stack scientific-computing scaffolding.

## Repository structure

- `python/` — electronic and photochemical material screening, derived metrics, and provenance manifests
- `r/` — replicate device-like performance and stability summaries
- `julia/` — photon, band-gap, and transport calculations
- `fortran/` — compact semiconductor metric kernel
- `rust/` — electronic materials dataset manifest validator
- `go/` — CSV audit utility for semiconductor records
- `c/` — photon energy and conductivity utility
- `cpp/` — electronic material candidate classifier
- `sql/` — schema and queries for materials, devices, photostability, spectroscopy, and lifecycle notes
- `docs/` — methodology, reproducibility, and responsible-use notes
- `data/` — synthetic educational electronic and photochemical materials data
- `notebooks/` — notebook scaffolds and report notes
- `outputs/` — generated tables, reports, figures, and manifests

## Responsible-use note

All data in this folder are synthetic and educational. These workflows are not validated for device certification, photovoltaic claims, photochemical safety, procurement, environmental claims, industrial process design, or regulatory use.
