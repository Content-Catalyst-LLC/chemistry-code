# Methodology Notes

## Purpose

This repository demonstrates reproducible computational scaffolding for physical chemistry and the chemical interpretation of matter.

## Core Relationships

Gibbs free energy:

G = H - T S

Equilibrium free energy:

Delta G = Delta G standard + R T ln Q

Standard free energy and equilibrium constant:

Delta G standard = -R T ln K

Arrhenius equation:

k = A exp(-Ea / R T)

Boltzmann population:

p_i = exp(-E_i / kB T) / Z

Diffusive flux:

J = -D dc/dx

Electrochemical free energy:

Delta G = -n F E

Nernst equation:

E = E0 - R T / n F ln Q

## Scientific Caution

These examples use simplified educational assumptions. Real physical chemistry requires validated models, units, calibrated measurements, solvent and activity assumptions, numerical diagnostics, uncertainty analysis, and expert interpretation.
