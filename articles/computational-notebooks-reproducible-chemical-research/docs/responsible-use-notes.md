# Responsible Use Notes

Computational notebooks can strengthen chemical reproducibility, but they do not replace chemical judgment, laboratory quality systems, safety review, or regulatory validation.

## This folder

The data and scripts in this article folder are synthetic and educational. They are appropriate for demonstrating reproducible workflows, provenance manifests, calibration summaries, and multi-language scientific-computing structure.

## Do not use this folder for

- clinical decision-making;
- environmental-compliance reporting;
- regulatory submissions;
- industrial batch release;
- hazardous-chemistry operations;
- safety-critical chemical risk decisions.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- connect instrument-export files to immutable raw-data storage;
- use certified reference-material metadata where applicable;
- preserve calibration certificates and standard-preparation records;
- include uncertainty budgets;
- validate software under the relevant laboratory quality system;
- document manual interventions and excluded observations;
- maintain access control for sensitive or proprietary records.
