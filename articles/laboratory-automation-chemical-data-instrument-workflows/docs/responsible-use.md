# Responsible Use Notes

Laboratory automation can support high-throughput and reproducible chemistry, but automated outputs are not automatically valid scientific or regulatory evidence.

## Do not use this folder for

- clinical diagnosis or treatment;
- environmental compliance reporting;
- forensic interpretation;
- pharmaceutical release;
- industrial quality certification;
- safety-critical chemical decisions;
- regulated laboratory audit submissions.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- connect to validated LIMS, ELN, SDMS, and instrument-control systems;
- preserve raw vendor files and open-format exports where possible;
- use stable sample identifiers and container identifiers;
- version instrument methods and data-processing workflows;
- record calibration, QC, maintenance, and system suitability records;
- preserve audit trails for manual overrides and reprocessing;
- test data pipelines with known standards and failure cases;
- document human approval and accountability.
