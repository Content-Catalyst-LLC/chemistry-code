# Responsible Use Notes

This repository is educational.

Do not use these scripts for:

- industrial reactor design
- combustion safety decisions
- hazardous reaction scale-up
- environmental compliance reporting
- pharmaceutical stability claims
- battery safety models
- medical or clinical decisions
- regulatory submissions
- laboratory procedures without professional review

Professional chemical practice requires validated mechanisms, trained personnel, calibrated measurement, uncertainty analysis, safety review, and regulatory compliance where applicable.
