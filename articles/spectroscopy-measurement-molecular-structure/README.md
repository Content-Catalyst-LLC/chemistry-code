# Spectroscopy and the Measurement of Molecular Structure

This article folder supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article **Spectroscopy and the Measurement of Molecular Structure**.

The workflows use synthetic educational data to demonstrate spectroscopic peak interpretation, photon-energy conversion, UV-visible calibration, IR functional-region assignment, NMR signal summarization, provenance documentation, and multi-language scientific-computing scaffolding.

## Repository structure

- `python/` — spectral peak assignment, energy conversion, UV-visible calibration, and provenance workflows
- `r/` — calibration regression, replicate summaries, and peak statistics
- `julia/` — wavelength, wavenumber, frequency, and photon-energy conversions
- `fortran/` — compact spectral energy conversion kernel
- `rust/` — spectral manifest validator
- `go/` — CSV audit utility for spectral records
- `c/` — wavelength-to-energy and wavenumber conversion utility
- `cpp/` — educational IR functional-region classifier
- `sql/` — schema and queries for spectroscopic measurements and assignments
- `docs/` — methodology, safety, and reproducibility notes
- `data/` — synthetic educational spectroscopic data
- `notebooks/` — notebook scaffolds and report notes
- `outputs/` — generated tables, reports, figures, and manifests

## Responsible-use note

All data in this folder are synthetic and educational. These workflows are not validated for clinical, forensic, environmental-compliance, pharmaceutical-release, industrial-quality, or safety-critical chemical decisions.
