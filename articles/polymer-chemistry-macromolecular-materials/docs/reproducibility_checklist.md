# Polymer Chemistry Reproducibility Checklist

- Record monomer identity, polymerization route, catalyst, initiator, solvent, and reaction conditions.
- Report molar mass averages, dispersity, and measurement method.
- Preserve processing history, thermal treatment, humidity conditioning, and post-treatment.
- Record additives, fillers, stabilizers, residual monomers, and leachable concerns where relevant.
- Link properties to specific batches, specimens, and test conditions.
- Include replicate measurements and uncertainty.
- Preserve raw and processed characterization data.
- Document degradation, aging, and lifecycle assumptions.
- Distinguish screening results from validated application readiness.
