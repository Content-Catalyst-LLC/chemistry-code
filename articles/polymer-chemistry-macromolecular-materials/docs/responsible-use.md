# Responsible Use Notes

Polymer evidence can influence packaging, healthcare, water systems, electronics, transportation, construction, agriculture, textiles, and environmental claims. The examples in this folder are not validated for those purposes.

## Do not use this folder for

- engineering certification;
- food-contact or medical-device decisions;
- biodegradation or compostability claims;
- recycling claims;
- regulated product qualification;
- safety-critical polymer selection;
- environmental marketing claims.

## Recommended real-world extensions

- preserve raw characterization data and processing history;
- report molar mass, dispersity, architecture, additives, and residual monomer;
- include replicate measurements and uncertainty;
- evaluate aging, weathering, creep, fatigue, chemical exposure, and degradation;
- distinguish biodegradation from fragmentation;
- test recycling pathways in realistic collection and processing systems;
- document extractables, leachables, stabilizers, and additives where safety matters.
