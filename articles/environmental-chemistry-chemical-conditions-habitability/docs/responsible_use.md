# Responsible Use Notes

These files are educational examples for chemistry communication, computational learning, and reproducible workflow design.

They should not be used as:

- regulatory compliance tools
- emergency-response guidance
- public-health advisories
- legal evidence
- site-remediation designs
- medical or toxicological diagnoses
- drinking-water safety determinations
- occupational exposure determinations

Environmental chemistry decisions can affect health, livelihoods, ecosystems, property, and public trust. Real applications require qualified experts, validated methods, legally appropriate standards, uncertainty analysis, and transparent communication with affected communities.

The examples intentionally use synthetic data.
