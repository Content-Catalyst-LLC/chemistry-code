# Full-Stack Computational Extensions

This folder extends the advanced Environmental Chemistry and the Chemical Conditions of Habitability repository beyond Python and SQL.

## Added languages

- Julia — multimedia partitioning, uncertainty, and habitability pressure modeling
- C — low-level Kd, retardation, Henry tendency, and decay kernels
- C++ — compartment-level habitability screening and source-pathway-receptor logic
- Fortran — numerical first-order decay and retardation modeling
- Rust — safe systems-style environmental chemistry screening
- Go — lightweight compartment and pathway summary analytics
- R — statistical summaries for compartment, analyte class, and pressure indicators

## Responsible-use boundary

All examples are synthetic and educational. They do not provide regulatory compliance, public-health advice, environmental forensic conclusions, site-remediation design, ecological risk assessment, drinking-water determinations, hazardous-waste determinations, legal evidence, property decisions, or operational monitoring guidance.
