# Computational Chemistry and Molecular Modeling

This article repository supports the Chemistry knowledge-series article:

Computational Chemistry and Molecular Modeling

The repository provides reproducible computational scaffolding for computational chemistry and molecular modeling, including molecular descriptor tables, molecular graph representations, conformer Boltzmann weighting, Lennard-Jones potential examples, Tanimoto similarity, reaction-energy tables, molecular dynamics toy models, SQL-backed molecular metadata, notebook scaffolding, and cross-language examples in Python, R, Julia, Fortran, Rust, Go, C, and C++.

No university affiliation is implied by this repository. The design goal is advanced reproducible computational rigor: clear assumptions, transparent calculations, synthetic educational data, provenance records, responsible-use notes, and reusable scientific workflow structure.

## Scientific Focus

The workflows demonstrate:

1. Molecular descriptor scaffolds.
2. Molecular graph metadata.
3. Conformer Boltzmann weighting.
4. Lennard-Jones potential calculations.
5. Tanimoto similarity.
6. Reaction-energy comparison.
7. Simple molecular dynamics update steps.
8. Descriptor-based property modeling scaffolds.
9. SQL-backed computational chemistry metadata.
10. Cross-language numerical reproducibility.

These examples are educational and methodological scaffolds. They are not validated quantum chemistry engines, molecular dynamics packages, drug-discovery systems, safety models, regulatory systems, or substitutes for professional computational chemistry practice.
